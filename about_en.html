About me

I am fundamentally trained as a civil engineer in technical physics and later received a PhD in theoretical chemistry from Lund University. After completing my doctoral studies, I continued my education to become a teacher in Malmö. Now, I work as a high school teacher in mathematics and physics in Lund.

Since I was a child, I have always had a great interest in drawing and painting, but it is only now that I have resumed it after primary school. At university, I came into contact with programming, which in many ways opens up a new world of possibilities for creativity.

Now, I combine my interest in drawing, mathematics, and programming by creating images with the help of a computer.
